DiT-based Music Generation

A diffusion-transformer framework for controllable music generation. This project explores melody-conditioned diffusion, long-range structure modeling, and qualitative audio analysis.

Paper arXiv Code Qualitative Results

Abstract

We propose a DiT-based music generation framework that extends latent diffusion models with transformer-based global context modeling. By incorporating melody-aware conditioning and structured training schedules, the model achieves improved musical coherence and controllability over long temporal horizons.

Method Overview

Our system builds upon a latent audio diffusion backbone and replaces the conventional U-Net architecture with a diffusion transformer (DiT). Conditioning signals such as text prompts and melody representations are injected through attention-based adapters.
